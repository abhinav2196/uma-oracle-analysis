## Key Insights

### 1. **Solana Dominates** 
- 50% of all crypto price predictions are for Solana
- This is notably higher than Bitcoin and Ethereum combined

### 2. **Equal Bitcoin & Ethereum Interest**
- Bitcoin and Ethereum have almost identical query counts (~1,850 each)
- Both represent 25% of total queries

### 3. **Trading Pair Format**
- Virtually all queries reference specific trading pairs (USDT or USD)
- Format: "Will the price of [ASSET] be above/below $[PRICE]..."
- Often include specific timestamps (e.g., "September 4 at 12AM ET")

### 4. **Price Threshold Queries**
- Most queries ask about price crossing specific thresholds
- Very few (<1%) are open-ended price queries
